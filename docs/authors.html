Definitions

These counts intentionally overlap in places. They are quick operational counters, not mutually exclusive states.

  • Amount open = open, non-draft, tracked PRs authored by that user.
  • Amount ready to merge = passes the repo-specific merge rules from the tracked profile config.
  • Amount blocked by CI/checks = at least one required configured check is failing.
  • Amount waiting for review = approvals are still below the repo threshold and there are no active changes requested.
